# Area 01 Blue Hole Atlas

Date: 2026-06-27

Purpose: give the first major dive region a visible map-shaped target before more local route issues are opened.

Map artifact:

- `docs/planning/maps/area_01_blue_hole_exploration_atlas_v1.svg`

## Decision

The Hollow Reef / Wide Chamber review map is not the full map. It represents one lower-route branch inside Area 01.

Area 01 should feel closer to a Dave-the-Diver-style side-view exploration region: a broad vertical ocean space with lateral routes, recognizable depth bands, stable landmarks, optional pockets, locked promises, and a few dangerous or creature-inhabited lanes.

The current Hollow Reef / Wide Chamber work is approximately one branch cluster of that region, not the final size target.

## Draft Area 01 Shape

Area 01 should eventually include:

- Surface base and moonpool extraction.
- Shallow starter descent and resource learning.
- Shell Reef bank route for safer cargo returns.
- Thermal Vent clue route.
- East Shelf side route and pocket.
- Pressure Wreck / Signal Cache promise.
- Shelf Drop, Blue Chimney, Silt Vein, Blackwater, and Dusk continuation.
- Lantern Ray and Gulper-style creature route reads.
- Hollow Reef side cave.
- Wide Reef Chamber as the first larger open-water room.
- Future locked deep route and future hunting cave promise.

The map should be big enough that side-to-side movement matters. A player should not feel like they only swim down a tube.

## Implementation Meaning

This atlas is a planning artifact, not an in-game minimap.

Use it to judge whether future issues are expanding the game in the right direction:

- Are we adding places, branches, and reasons to choose routes?
- Are new landmarks placed in a coherent regional topology?
- Are locked promises visible without becoming checklist objectives?
- Are we avoiding another long run of tiny polish issues that leave the playable map feeling unchanged?

## Current Slice Marker

The atlas marks Hollow Reef / Wide Chamber as the current active branch cluster.

That slice should become a satisfying local route, but the next major route-growth target should connect it to a broader Area 01 structure instead of treating it as the whole level.

## Recommended Next Use

Before opening another large issue batch, create issues that turn this atlas into playable breadth:

- Add one or two side pockets branching from the Wide Reef Chamber.
- Add a future locked transition beyond the chamber.
- Add one creature or hazard lane that uses the chamber space.
- Add one meaningful resource or knowledge payoff that makes the branch worth revisiting.
- Add cheap route-state or screenshot evidence only where the new space changes camera, layout, or readability.
